## Brand Direction

### Core Impression

The portfolio should feel precise, calm, senior, technically credible, visually disciplined, easy to scan, and purposeful rather than decorative.

### Brand Voice

Write as an established technical brand, not as a freelancer asking to be trusted.

Avoid:

- I can help with
- I can build
- I can investigate
- I can work across
- I am able to
- Whether you need
- My services include

Prefer:

- Built for
- Designed to
- From X to Y
- End-to-end
- Reliable by design
- Clear, maintainable, and ready to scale
- Existing systems, improved without unnecessary disruption
- Technical complexity translated into dependable products

Use first person selectively. Most service copy should describe what the work delivers rather than repeatedly explain what Akash is capable of doing.

# Selected Work

## Ecommerce Delivery Intelligence Platform

### Category

Ecommerce · Backend Architecture · Production Systems

### Headline

# Delivery promises made dependable

### Challenge

A large ecommerce business needed one reliable source of truth for delivery estimates across products, warehouses, locations, couriers, holidays, manufacturing delays, stock status, and express-delivery rules.

The existing process produced delivery-date accuracy of only around 30%, creating customer confusion, avoidable support calls, and unnecessary operational cost.

### Delivery

A centralized delivery-estimation service combined operational data and business rules from courier, inventory, ecommerce, and warehouse systems.

The platform also introduced Redis-backed caching, administrative data uploads, version history, backup and restore, and automated out-of-stock alerts.

### Results

- Delivery-date accuracy improved from approximately 30% to 95%
- Customer calls reduced by approximately 10%
- Around INR 1 crore in annual cost savings
- Approximately 1.45 million requests supported during the Diwali period
- Peak traffic handled at roughly 199% above the previous month

### Capabilities

Backend architecture, API design, business-rule modelling, external integrations, caching, production scaling, observability, and long-term maintenance.

# UI/UX Direction

## Visual Style

Use:

- Near-black or deep-charcoal background
- Slightly lighter surface layers
- Soft off-white primary text
- Muted cool-grey secondary text
- Low-contrast borders
- One restrained cold accent such as icy blue, steel blue, or desaturated cyan

Avoid:

- Bright gradients
- Neon cyberpunk styling
- Excessive shadows
- Decorative blobs
- Fake terminal aesthetics
- Code rain
- Heavy glassmorphism
- Multiple accent colours

## Typography

Use one clean sans-serif family throughout.

Recommended:

- Geist
- Inter
- IBM Plex Sans
- Manrope

Typography should carry most of the visual identity.

## Layout

- Main container: approximately 1120–1200px
- Reading width: approximately 650–760px
- Clear vertical rhythm
- Strong alignment
- Spacious but not wasteful
- Responsive grids
- Subtle section boundaries

## Interaction Design

Allowed:

- Soft fade and upward entrance on first view
- Small hover transitions
- Button movement of 1–2px
- Link underline or colour transitions
- Project-card border or surface shifts
- Smooth anchor scrolling
- Reduced-motion support

Avoid:

- Scroll-jacking
- Parallax
- Cursor-following effects
- Magnetic buttons
- Constant moving backgrounds
- Long intro animations
- Anything that delays access to content

## Accessibility

Required:

- Semantic HTML
- Keyboard navigation
- Visible focus states
- Proper colour contrast
- Descriptive links
- Form labels
- Reduced-motion support
- Correct heading hierarchy
- Alt text for meaningful images

# Final Standard

The site should not try to prove experimental visual-design skill.

It should prove the ability to design and build a polished, usable, technically credible web product with restraint, hierarchy, and judgment.

Every design decision should improve at least one of the following:

- Hierarchy
- Readability
- Comprehension
- Navigation
- Trust
- Interaction feedback

If it improves none of them, remove it.
